About timecodes & the ONNX stride caveat

Phone times come from the model's greedy alignment. For the ONNX CTC backend the frame stride is approximated (~20 ms/frame; ~40 ms for the ONNX transducer); the PyTorch backend uses exact encoder lengths. Treat ONNX timings as approximate and validate against a reference. See the project README, section “Caution: ONNX length heuristic”.

100% client-side — no audio or transcript leaves your browser.
